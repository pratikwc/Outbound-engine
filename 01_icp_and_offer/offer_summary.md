## Product Modules (match to prospect's pain when writing copy)

### 1. AI Screening & Shortlisting
**Target dept/titles**: People & HR — HR Manager, Hiring Manager, Recruiter

**Pain**
- Screening 50+ CVs per role every week is eating the team's time
- Shortlisting is inconsistent — depends on who's doing it that day

**Value props**
- AI reviews every CV, assignment, and video submission and hands over a ranked shortlist
- Shortlisting quality improves by 50% and takes minutes instead of days

**Use cases**
- Running a high-volume role and getting a clean top-10 list before the hiring manager opens their laptop
- Replacing one-way video screening that used to take 3 hours manually

**Killer questions**
- How long does it currently take your team to go from applications to a shortlist?
- How consistent is your screening criteria across different roles?

**Why now**: Volume of applicants has gone up but the team size hasn't

---

### 2. AI Sourcing & Job Distribution
**Target dept/titles**: Talent Acquisition — Recruiter, Talent Acquisition Manager, HR Lead

**Pain**
- Manually posting to job boards takes hours and still misses good candidates
- Sourcing is reactive — waiting for applicants instead of going after talent

**Value props**
- Searches 8M+ profiles across 50+ channels automatically
- Publishes to 1,000+ job boards in one click

**Use cases**
- Launching a new role and getting a full pipeline within 24 hours
- Finding passive candidates who never applied but match the role perfectly

**Killer questions**
- How many channels are you currently posting to and how long does it take?
- When did you last hire someone you proactively sourced vs someone who applied?

**Why now**: Active roles are sitting open because the pipeline is too thin

---

### 3. Interview Scheduling & Management
**Target dept/titles**: People & HR / Operations — HR Coordinator, Recruiter, Office Manager

**Pain**
- Scheduling interviews is a back and forth that takes days
- No structured feedback from interviewers — everyone shares notes differently

**Value props**
- Candidates self-schedule, links are auto-generated, AI notetaker joins every call
- Structured debrief is ready before the panel even meets

**Use cases**
- Scheduling 20+ interviews in a week without anyone touching a calendar
- Getting consistent interviewer feedback without chasing people on Slack

**Killer questions**
- How many hours a week does your team spend on interview coordination?
- How do you currently collect and compare feedback from multiple interviewers?

**Why now**: Interview delays are causing good candidates to drop off mid-process

---

### 4. Offer & Onboarding Automation
**Target dept/titles**: HR / Legal / Operations — HR Manager, People Ops, COO

**Pain**
- Offer letters take days to go out after a decision is made
- Onboarding is manual — documents, signatures, and setup all done separately

**Value props**
- Offer generated, approved, signed, and onboarding triggered in one automated flow
- No delays between "we want to hire them" and "they're starting"

**Use cases**
- Sending an offer the same day a hiring decision is made
- Automatically kicking off onboarding the moment the offer is signed

**Killer questions**
- How long does it currently take from decision to offer letter?
- How many people are involved in getting a new hire's paperwork done?

**Why now**: Candidates are accepting competing offers while waiting for paperwork

---

### 5. Performance & Pipeline Analytics
**Target dept/titles**: HR / Leadership — Head of HR, CHRO, Founder, COO

**Pain**
- No visibility into where hiring is getting stuck
- Can't answer basic questions like time-to-hire or source quality without pulling reports manually

**Value props**
- Ask anything about your pipeline in plain language — AI generates the report instantly
- See time-to-hire by role, department, and source without touching a spreadsheet

**Use cases**
- Weekly hiring review with leadership where every answer comes from live data
- Identifying that one interview stage is causing 40% of candidate drop-off

**Killer questions**
- Do you know right now which of your open roles is most at risk of missing its target date?
- How long does it take you to pull a hiring report today?

**Why now**: Leadership is asking for hiring metrics and there's no clean way to get them

### 6. Whitecarrot AI Recruiter (Full Suite / Default)
**Target dept/titles**: People & HR / Founding Team — Head of HR, HR Manager, Founder, CEO

**Pain**

**Value props**

- Running 10+ open roles simultaneously without adding headcount

**Killer questions**

**Why now**: Team is scaling and the current process can't keep up with the volume
