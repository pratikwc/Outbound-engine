# Case Studies

Use these as social proof in email copy. One sentence max per email — numbers beat adjectives.

---

## Case Study 1: Alabbar Enterprises — Dubai, UAE

**Company**: Alabbar Enterprises · Dubai, UAE · 1,250 employees · Hospitality/HoReCA · diversified enterprise (Alabbar Enterprisesgroup.com)

**Contact**: Tariq, Head of Recruitment (17 years experience, previously used Oracle, SAP, Team Tailor)

**Before**
- 8 recruiters handling ~350 hires/year
- 3 disconnected systems not talking to each other
- 42 hours of admin work per recruiter per week
- 44-day average time-to-hire

**After (with Whitecarrot)**
- 3 recruiters handling 850 hires/year
- 2.3× more hires with 60% fewer recruiters
- Admin time cut from 42 hours → 6 hours (86% reduction)
- Time-to-hire: 44 days → 2 days (with AI model)
- Platform usage: 350+ jobs published/month · 5,600+ candidate views/quarter · 102K+ active profiles

**Key quotes (for email use)**
- "Hiring 500 plus employees — you cannot rely on two or three recruiters. But we did."
- "We are relying on the system 100%."
- "They can work with Whitecarrot blindly. They can trust it."
- "I'm having 15 plus years of experience — and this is the system I recommend."
- "It cannot force you to do something that you don't want to do." *(speaks to fear of rigid legacy software)*

**One-liner for cold email**: "We helped a Hospitality group in Dubai go from 8 recruiters doing 350 hires to 3 recruiters doing 850 — same team, 2.3× the output."

**Use when**: Prospect is in Hospitality, large enterprise, or mentions too few recruiters for volume of hiring

## Case Study 2: Beehive — UAE

**Company**: Beehive · UAE · FinTech/Financial Services

**Contact**: Rashi Verma (HR/People function)

**Before**
- Using BambooHR ATS — described as "very manual"
- CVs scattered across email, LinkedIn, Teams messages
- Fragmented process: no single source of truth
- ~45 days time to fill

**After (with Whitecarrot)**
- Single platform replacing all disconnected tools
- Significantly faster hiring process
- Hiring managers self-serve for junior/easy-to-fill roles (no recruiter needed)

**Key quotes (for email use)**

- "CVs were on email, some were coming in through LinkedIn, some were coming in through Teams messages."
- "AI is going to kill a lot of the admin, repetitive work that is a part of recruitment."
- "So it already does the AI CV review, which still so many other ATS platforms don't have."

**One-liner for cold email**: "A FinTech in UAE replaced BambooHR and scattered CVs across email/LinkedIn/Teams with one platform — hiring managers now self-serve for junior roles."

**Use when**: Prospect is using BambooHR/legacy ATS, mentions fragmented tools, or is in Financial Services

## How to Use in Emails

- **Max one sentence** — drop the number, not the story
- Match the case study industry to the prospect's industry when possible
- Alabbar Enterprises stat (2.3× hires, 60% fewer recruiters) is your strongest metric — use for volume/scale objections
- Beehive quote (CVs on email/LinkedIn/Teams) resonates with anyone still not centralised
- Never fabricate numbers or attribute quotes without confirming they're real
- **Always pair name + outcome** — the company name alone is not social proof
