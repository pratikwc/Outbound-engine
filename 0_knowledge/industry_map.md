# Industry Classification Map

The research agent uses this file to classify a prospect's company into the correct industry bucket. Get this right — it determines which social proof gets used in their email.

---

## How to Classify

**Step 1:** Check the company's LinkedIn industry field first.
**Step 2:** If LinkedIn is ambiguous or wrong, check: what kind of jobs does this company hire for? That tells you the real bucket.
**Step 3:** Match to one of the buckets below.
**Step 4:** Output the bucket name in the research brief so the outreach agent can pull the right case study from social_proof.md.

## Industry Buckets

---

### Bucket 1 — Hospitality / F&B (Horeca)
**LinkedIn nodes:** Hospitality, Food and Beverage Services
**Who goes here:** Hotels, resort chains, restaurant chains, cafe chains, catering companies, entertainment venues with F&B operations
**What they hire for:** Kitchen staff, chefs, waiters, reception, hotel operations, housekeeping, service staff
**WC clients:** Bidfood Middle East, Alabbar Enterprises, Chedi Hospitality, Daikan Hospitality Group, Ethos

---

### Bucket 2 — Retail / Wholesale / Distribution
**LinkedIn nodes:** Retail, Wholesale, Wholesale Import and Export, Consumer Services
**Who goes here:** Companies with physical stores, retail outlets, distribution networks, wholesale import/export, consumer service brands
**What they hire for:** Retail sales staff, store managers, warehouse staff, inventory managers, logistics, delivery, customer-facing staff
**WC clients:** SARA Group, Madi International, WOLFI'S, The Luxury Closet, The Petshop, Chemist Warehouse UAE,  (⚠️ verify)

---

### Bucket 3 — Financial Services
**LinkedIn nodes:** Financial Services, Banking, Investment Management, Venture Capital and Private Equity, FinTech
**Who goes here:** Banks, investment firms, financial technology companies, payment platforms, insurance
**What they hire for:** Analysts, relationship managers, compliance officers, tech/product roles
**WC clients:** Beehive, STARTRADER

---

### Bucket 4 — Technology
**LinkedIn nodes:** Software Development, IT Services and IT Consulting, Computer and Network Security, Technology Information and Internet
**Who goes here:** SaaS companies, tech startups, IT services firms, cybersecurity firms, AI companies, managed service providers
**What they hire for:** Engineers, product managers, designers, sales, support, security analysts
**WC clients:** Bayzat, AppliedAI, Penny Software, TPConnects Technologies, EuroTech ME, Help AG
**Note:** Cybersecurity firms (Help AG) go here — not a separate bucket.

---

### Bucket 5 — Real Estate / Construction / Architecture
**LinkedIn nodes:** Real Estate, Real Estate and Equipment Rental Services, Architecture and Planning, Construction, Civil Engineering
**Who goes here:** Property agencies, proptech companies, real estate developers, contractors, engineering firms, architecture firms, fit-out companies
**What they hire for:** Agents, brokers, property managers, engineers, architects, project managers, site staff
**WC clients:** PRYPCO, Ektis, Allsopp & Allsopp, Richmond Properties, ELECTRA, X-Architects
**Note:** Consulting firms that happen to work in real estate (QuadraBay, OAKTREE) go in Bucket 6, not here. Verify LinkedIn industry before placing.

### Bucket 6 — Professional Services
**LinkedIn nodes:** Business Consulting and Services, Management Consulting, Accounting, Legal Services, Staffing and Recruiting, Outsourcing and Offshoring
**Who goes here:** Consulting firms, advisory firms, accounting practices, law firms, professional services companies, background check firms

**WC clients:** Massar Business Solutions, Strive Consultants, ACM Limited, QuadraBay, RSM UAE, HAS Law Firm
**Note:** Accounting firms (RSM) and law firms (HAS) both go here — same hiring profile as consulting.

### Bucket 7 — Healthcare
**LinkedIn nodes:** Hospitals and Health Care, Medical Practices, Pharmaceutical Manufacturing, Biotechnology Research, Veterinary Services
**Who goes here:** Hospitals, clinics, dental chains, pharma distributors, healthcare tech companies, vet clinics
**What they hire for:** Doctors, nurses, clinical staff, pharmacists, vets, admin, healthcare operations
**WC clients:** TruDoc, Dr Joy Dental Clinics, Muller and Phipps, Getz Healthcare, Modern Vet
**Note:** Vet clinics (Modern Vet) go here — same clinical hiring pattern as dental/medical practices.

---

### Bucket 8 — Wellness & Entertainment
**LinkedIn nodes:** Wellness and Fitness Services, Entertainment Providers, Recreational Facilities
**Who goes here:** Gyms, fitness studios, entertainment venues, activity centres, sports facilities
**What they hire for:** Personal trainers, instructors, venue staff, operations
**WC clients:** Enhance Fitness, Institute for Healthier Living Abu Dhabi, BOUNCE

### Bucket 9 — Manufacturing
**LinkedIn nodes:** Packaging and Containers Manufacturing, Chemical Manufacturing, Food and Beverage Manufacturing, Industrial Automation, Oil and Gas
**Who goes here:** Manufacturers, industrial companies, chemical producers, packaging companies
**What they hire for:** Plant operators, engineers, quality control, supply chain, warehouse

---

### Bucket 10 — Other
**LinkedIn nodes:** Holding Companies, Ground Passenger Transportation, Advertising Services
**Who goes here:** Conglomerates, holding groups, transport companies, advertising agencies — companies that don't fit cleanly into any bucket above
**WC clients:** Ahmad Al-Muhaisini Holding, HalaTaxi, Umami Comms
**Social proof fallback:** Use Alabbar Enterprises (strongest stat, universally relatable). Note HalaTaxi ≠ Careem — never confuse the two.

## If You Can't Classify

1. Check the company's careers page — what kind of jobs are they posting?
2. Check their LinkedIn "About" section and description
3. If still unclear, classify as "Other" and flag in research brief
4. Never guess and assign confidently — a wrong industry = wrong social proof = lower response rate
