# Whitecarrot — Social Proof Bank

Client outcomes by industry. The outreach agent uses this to pick the right case study for each prospect.

**Rules (non-negotiable):**
- Always pair company name + outcome. Name alone = nothing.
- Volume stats (jobs published, hires logged, candidates reviewed) come from PostHog platform activity. They prove real usage — not ROI. Use for credibility.
- Full before/after ROI numbers exist only for **Alabbar Enterprises** and **Beehive**. Use those freely. Do not attribute ROI or time-to-hire numbers to any other client.
- Match the industry. A FinTech example on a hospitality prospect is lazy and it shows.
- One sentence max per email.
- HalaTaxi = our client. NOT Careem. Never say Careem.
