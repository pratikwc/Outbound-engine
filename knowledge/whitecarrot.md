## What Whitecarrot Does

Whitecarrot is AI recruitment software that puts the entire hiring process in one platform so teams spend time on decisions, not admin.

Most companies we work with are either:
- Managing hiring on spreadsheets, email, and WhatsApp — no system at all
- Using an ATS that doesn't actually save them time (BambooHR, Zoho, SAP) — still too manual, no AI

**What Whitecarrot covers end-to-end:**
Plan headcount → post jobs on 1,000+ boards (including LinkedIn via direct partnership) → AI screens and ranks CVs → schedule interviews → collect video responses → send offers → onboard paperlessly. No extra tools needed. One platform, full cycle.

## The Real Problems We Solve

Use these to frame pain in emails. All verified — do not fabricate numbers.

- **Too many CVs, too little review:** 900+ applications per high-volume role on average. Less than 10% ever get read. Good candidates get missed.
- **Hiring takes too long:** Average time to fill is 44 days. Roles sit open for 6 weeks while the business waits.
- **Backfills are chaos:** 90%+ of backfill hires miss the target hire date.
- **Hiring is expensive:** Agency fees run 8–15% of annual CTC per hire. 78% of employers in the region still use agencies.
- **Recruiters are stuck in admin:** 60%+ of a recruiter's week goes to tasks that don't require a human — formatting JDs, chasing interview slots, copying CVs, sending status updates.
- **No pipeline visibility:** CVs on email, feedback on WhatsApp, job posts on five boards, offer letters on Google Docs. No one has a real-time view.

**GCC-specific pain (use for all UAE/KSA contacts):**
Every expat hire triggers document collection, verification, and visa coordination. Most companies manage this over WhatsApp and email — manual, error-prone, compliance risk. Whitecarrot handles it inside the platform.

**KSA-specific pain (use only for KSA contacts):**
Companies must maintain Saudi national quotas under Nitaqat. Whitecarrot handles the document collection, nationality verification, and compliance paperwork for every Saudi national hire. Built in, not bolted on.

## Two Types of Hiring Roles — Context for Agent

**Easy-to-fill roles (high volume):** Too many CVs coming in. AI screening and ranking solves this. Whitecarrot posts to 1,000+ boards and ranks the shortlist automatically.

**Hard-to-fill roles (specialist, senior):** Finding the right person requires going out on the internet. Whitecarrot's AI headhunting agent can target candidates from LinkedIn and run outreach sequences to build a shortlist. *(Note: headhunting agent is in development — experimental, on roadmap. Mention as upcoming capability, not live feature.)*

**Rent-a-recruiter:** A service where clients can access a dedicated recruiter when needed. *(On roadmap, experimental — mention as upcoming capability only.)*
