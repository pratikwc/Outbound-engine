## Why the free audit works
Lowest friction entry for No ATS companies — you're validating their problem, not selling a product. The review conversation naturally surfaces whether Whitecarrot is a fit. Companies with visible hiring pain (multiple open roles on LinkedIn) are the best targets for this angle.
