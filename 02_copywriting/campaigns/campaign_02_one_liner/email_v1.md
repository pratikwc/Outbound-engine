# Campaign 02 — Strategy 2 (One-Liner)
**Persona**: B — TA Champion (Talent Acquisition Manager / Director / Head of TA)
**Segment**: No ATS (328 companies)
**Angle**: Curiosity trigger — ask about their own screening time, say almost nothing, make them reply

## Why the one-liner works for Persona B
TA people live in the screening process daily. Asking them directly about their time forces self-reflection — either they reply with the number (warm conversation starts) or they ignore it. Either way you get a clean signal fast with zero wasted words.
