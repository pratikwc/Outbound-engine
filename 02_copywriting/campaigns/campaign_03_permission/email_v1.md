## Why permission works for Persona C
HR Managers juggle recruiting, compliance, onboarding, and performance reviews simultaneously. They're protective of their inbox. Asking permission signals respect and immediately differentiates from every pitch email that leads with "I wanted to reach out." Even a "not interested" reply is a clean signal.
