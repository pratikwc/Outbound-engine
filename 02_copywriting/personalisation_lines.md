# Personalisation Lines — Industry Fallback Library

Used when the research agent can't find a specific signal for a person or company. Pick the line that fits their industry. One sentence only, first line of the email.

Rules:
- Never mention a specific job role or job posting
- Never make up facts
- Under 15 words preferred
- Don't start with "I"
- These are FALLBACKS — a real signal from research always beats these

---

## Hospitality

- "Hospitality teams in UAE are often running hiring across WhatsApp, email, and a spreadsheet — no single pipeline."
- "High turnover in hospitality means the hiring process never really stops — it just gets more chaotic."
- "Most hotel and F&B operators at {{company_name}}'s scale are still filling roles reactively, not proactively."
