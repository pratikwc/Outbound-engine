# CTA Bank

All CTA variants tested or available. Note which campaigns use each.

## Soft CTAs (lower friction — better for cold)
| CTA | Campaign Used In | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Worth a 15-min chat? | campaign_01 | Simple, non-pushy |
| Open to a 15-min call? | campaign_01 v2 | Slightly softer |
| Want me to send over more detail? | — | Good for value emails |
| Is this relevant to what you're working on? | campaign_03 | Permission angle |

## Hard CTAs (higher intent signal required)
| CTA | Campaign Used In | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Here's my Calendly — pick a time that works: [link] | — | Use in follow-up 3 or 4 |
| Can I send you a 2-min Loom walkthrough? | — | Alternative to call |

## Rules
- Never use "I'd love to..." or "I wanted to reach out"
- One CTA per email only
- Match CTA friction to email number (softer early, harder later)
