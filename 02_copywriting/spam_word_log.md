## General Spam Triggers to Avoid (from Instantly masterclass)
- "Free" (in subject line)
- "Guaranteed"
- "Limited time"
- "Click here"
- "Unsubscribe" (in body — let Instantly handle)
- ALL CAPS anywhere
- Excessive punctuation (!!!, ???)
- "Dear [Name]" openers

## How to Use
- After every spam check in Instantly, log any new flags here
- If a word is flagged, don't just remove it — find a human alternative and log what you used instead
