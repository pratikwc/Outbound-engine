# Handover Rules

When to escalate to Pratik instead of responding directly.

**Default mode: Agent drafts, Pratik reviews. Nothing sends without Pratik's eyes on it.**

---

## Always Escalate (do not reply without Pratik reviewing)

| Signal | Why |
|---|---|
| Prospect requests pricing | Only Pratik handles pricing — no numbers in email |
| Prospect mentions a competitor by name | Needs careful, deliberate response |
| Prospect indicates readiness to buy | Don't let a hot lead go cold waiting on agent |
| Prospect asks a technical question the agent can't answer | Out of scope — hand to Pratik |
| Prospect is a senior decision-maker (CHRO, CEO) | High-value — Pratik should be in the loop |
| Prospect requests a custom demo or enterprise setup | Non-standard deal — needs human judgment |
| Reply has negative or hostile tone | Don't risk making it worse |
| Legal, compliance, or data privacy question | Out of scope for reply agent |
| Reply is ambiguous and could go either way | Default to escalating when unsure |

---

## Scheduling Protocol

**Step 1 — Positive reply:** Propose 2–3 specific time slots in Dubai time first. Do not immediately share the Calendly link.

Example wording: *"Would Tuesday at 3pm or Wednesday at 5pm Dubai Time work for a quick 20-minute call?"*

**Step 2 — No response to proposed slots:** Then share the Calendly link.

**Step 3 — Prospect asks for the link directly:** Share it immediately.

**Calendly link**: https://meetings.hubspot.com/mridul-bajoria

---

## When NOT to Send Any Reply

- Prospect unsubscribed or said "remove me" — mark as unsubscribed only, no response
- OOO auto-reply — no action, pause sequence
- If you're unsure whether it's a real reply or bot/spam — escalate

---

## After a Decline

Respond graciously. Suggest reconnecting in 2–3 months. Do not follow up more than once after a decline.

---

## How to Flag an Escalation

1. Mark the reply in Unibox — do not draft a response
2. Notify Pratik immediately (Slack or tag)
3. Do not send any response until Pratik confirms
