# WC Outbound — Instantly Engine

## Project Goal
Book meetings with HR leaders and decision-makers at UAE/KSA companies via cold email on Instantly.ai.

## ICP Definition
- **Title**: HR Director, CHRO, VP People, Talent Acquisition Lead, Head of HR
- **Company size**: 100–500 employees (sweet spot), 501–1,000 (high value)
- **Location**: UAE and KSA
- **Funding stage**: Growth-stage, bootstrapped profitable, PE-backed
- **Buying trigger**: Scaling headcount fast, no ATS or using spreadsheets/email to manage hiring

## Whitecarrot Offer Summary
Whitecarrot is an AI recruitment software that centralises the hiring pipeline, saves recruiter time, and integrates with tools like BambooHR. Platform pays for itself in under 11 months on recruiter time savings alone.

## Brand Voice Rules

- No buzzwords: never use "skyrocket", "leverage", "synergy", "game-changer"

- Write like a human, not a template
