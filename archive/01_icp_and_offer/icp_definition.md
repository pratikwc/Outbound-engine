# ICP Definition

## Segment 1: "No ATS" Companies
- Currently managing hiring via spreadsheets, email, or WhatsApp
- Pain: no pipeline visibility, losing candidates, slow time-to-hire
- Buying trigger: headcount growth, new HR hire, recent bad hire experience
- Best titles to target: HR Manager, HR Director, CHRO, Head of People

## Segment 2: "Has ATS" Companies
- Using legacy ATS (SAP SuccessFactors, Oracle, old Taleo installs) — clunky and underused
- Pain: tool not adopted by hiring managers, poor reporting, no AI features
- Buying trigger: contract renewal coming up, new HR leadership, poor recruiter experience
- Best titles to target: VP People, CHRO, Talent Acquisition Lead

## Company Size
| Size | Priority | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 100–200 | High | Decision-maker is usually 1 person, fast cycle |
| 201–500 | High (sweet spot) | Enough hiring volume to feel the pain acutely |
| 501–1,000 | High value | Longer deal cycle, higher ACV |
| <100 | Low | Too small, hiring volume too low |

## Funding Stage
- Growth-stage (Series A–C): scaling fast, need process
- Bootstrapped profitable: cost-conscious, ROI matters
- PE-backed: process and compliance focus

## Job Titles to Target
- CHRO / Chief People Officer
- VP People / VP HR
- HR Director
- Head of Talent / Talent Acquisition Lead
- HR Manager (at smaller companies where they are the decision-maker)

## Buying Triggers (signals to personalise around)

**General:**
- LinkedIn: 5+ open roles visible simultaneously
- Recent company funding or expansion to new markets
- New HR leader joined in last 6 months
- Job posting for "Recruiter" or "TA Manager" — they feel the pain now
- Using BambooHR, Oracle, SAP, Taleo, Workday — ripe for replacement or supplement

**KSA-specific:**
- Company is close to or at risk of breaching Nitaqat (Saudization) quotas — especially construction, hospitality, retail
- Rapid headcount growth in KSA where expat hire rate is high (document verification + visa is a recurring headache)
- Company just received or is pursuing a Vision 2030-linked government contract — hiring at scale, with Saudi national requirements
- HR team posting roles that include "Saudization" or "Nationalization" in the JD — active compliance pressure

## Sales Framing Note
- In **sales conversations and email copy**: Whitecarrot is an *AI recruitment software*
- In **marketing/website**: Whitecarrot is an *AI hiring agent* (2–3 months ahead positioning)
- Do not use the "AI hiring agent" framing in cold email — prospects are in UAE/KSA where trust is built on proof, not positioning
