## 1. What Whitecarrot Does

Whitecarrot is AI recruitment software that puts your entire hiring process in one place and uses AI to do the heavy lifting - so your team spends time on decisions, not admin.

Most companies we work with are either:
- Running hiring on spreadsheets, email, and WhatsApp - no system at all
- Using an ATS that doesn't actually save them time (BambooHR, Zoho, SAP) - still too manual

Whitecarrot covers the full cycle: plan headcount → post jobs on 1,000+ boards → AI screens and ranks CVs → schedule interviews → send offers → onboard paperlessly. No extra tools needed.

**For GCC companies specifically**, Whitecarrot also handles:
- **Document verification** — collect and verify candidate documents in the platform, no back-and-forth over email
- **Visa coordination** — built into the onboarding flow, not a separate process
- **Arabic Suport** — tracks Saudi vs. non-Saudi hires against Nitaqat quotas so companies always know where they stand, without a separate spreadsheet

This is why we're not a generic ATS. Most US/global vendors don't touch these. For companies in KSA especially, this is the difference between a tool that fits and one that needs a workaround for every hire.

We work with both knowledge worker companies (tech, finance, professional services) and front-line worker companies (hospitality, retail, construction, logistics). 100s of companies, 1000+ recruiters across the region use us.

## 2. The Real Problems We Solve

These are the actual numbers from the market - use them to frame pain in emails.

- **Too many CVs, too little review**: 900+ applications per high volume role on average. Less than 10% ever get read. Good candidates get missed because the team runs out of time.
- **Hiring takes too long**: Average time to fill is 44 days. That means roles sit open for 6 weeks while the business waits.
- **Backfills are chaos**: 90%+ of backfill hires miss the target hire date. Someone leaves, the business is understaffed, and the replacement process drags.
- **Hiring is expensive**: Cost of hire is ~3x monthly salary when you factor in agency fees (8–15% of CTC) and recruiter time.
- **Recruiters are stuck in admin**: 60%+ of a recruiter's week goes to tasks that have nothing to do with actually hiring - formatting JDs, chasing interview slots, copying CVs between systems, sending status updates.
- **GCC-specific: document and visa chaos** (use for UAE/KSA companies): Every expat hire triggers a chain of document collection, verification, and visa coordination. Most companies manage this over WhatsApp and email — it's manual, error-prone, and a compliance risk. Whitecarrot handles this inside the same platform.
- **KSA-specific: Saudization compliance** (use only for KSA companies): Companies are required to maintain Saudi national quotas under Nitaqat. Whitecarrot supports this procedurally — the platform handles the document collection, nationality verification, and compliance paperwork that comes with every Saudi national hire. The process is built in, not bolted on.

## 8. Email Writing Rules (apply every time)

**Structure**: Personalization → Pain → Offer + Social proof → CTA

**Personalization:**
- 1 sentence max - opens the door, doesn't close the deal
- Reference growth, hiring volume, team expansion, or industry context
- **Never mention a specific job role they're hiring for** - it implies Whitecarrot only helps with one role. We cover all roles, end-to-end.

**Offer framing:**
- "We help [company type] manage all their candidates in one place and use AI to make hiring a lot faster."
- Then immediately follow with social proof from their industry

**Tone rules (non-negotiable):**
- Under 15 seconds to read silently - no exceptions
- No buzzwords: never write "skyrocket", "leverage", "synergy", "game-changer", "cutting-edge", "next-gen"
- No long intros. Get to the point in line 1.
- Write like a human sending one email, not a template going to 500 people
