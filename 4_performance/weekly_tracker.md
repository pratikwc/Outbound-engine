## Status Key
- **Kill**: Under 2% reply rate after 50+ sends
- **Test More**: 2–5% reply rate — keep running, try one variable change
- **Scale**: Above 5% reply rate — increase send volume, document in winning_campaigns.md

## Notes
- Log numbers as they come in — don't wait for perfect data
- If open rate is low (<30%), problem is subject line or deliverability
- If open rate is high but reply rate is low (<2%), problem is the body copy or CTA
